Goat Cheese Stuffed Mushrooms

Freshyly baked goat cheese stuffed mushrooms.

Really delicious comfort food perfect for any occasion and any cheese lover.

Ingredients

  • Mushrooms - 5oz
  • Shallot (purple) - 1 small
  • Goat Cheese - 4oz

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. Finely chop shallot and mix with goat cheese.
  3. Bcoop and press mixture into cleaned and de-stemmed mushrooms.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes or until tops of goat cheese become toasty brwon.
